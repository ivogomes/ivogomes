      How to use TapScore

      Prop your phone up courtside and score the whole match with taps — it keeps the points, games, sets and serve for you.

      Tap to score

      Tap anywhere on a player's side of the screen to give them the point. Points roll up into games and sets automatically. Made a mistake? Hit Undo.

      Tap the ball to switch serve

      The ball next to a name marks who's serving. It moves on its own as games go by — but if it ever gets out of sync, just tap it on the correct side to fix it.

      Score with a Bluetooth clicker

      Pair any Bluetooth clicker or remote that types like a keyboard (a camera shutter or presentation remote works), then set the phone aside where everyone can see the score:

      • 1 click Point for your team
      • 2 clicks Point for the opponents
      • Hold Undo the last point

      Hear the score out loud

      Tap the speaker button in the bottom bar to have each point called out. Pair a Bluetooth speaker and everyone hears the running score — no need to glance at the phone.

      iPhone tip: if the voice sounds robotic, download a better one in Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices → English (pick an “Enhanced” or “Premium” voice), then choose it under Settings → Announcer voice in TapScore.
