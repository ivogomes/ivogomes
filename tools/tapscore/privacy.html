TapScore

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

The short version: TapScore does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no ads. Your matches and settings stay on your device.

Who this applies to

This policy covers the TapScore mobile apps (iOS and Android), the TapScore apps for Apple Watch and Wear OS, and the TapScore web app. Throughout this policy, “TapScore”, “we”, and “us” refer to the developer, Ivo Gomes.

What we collect

Nothing. TapScore has no user accounts and no sign-in. We do not collect personal information, contacts, location, photos, or any usage analytics.

The scores, match settings, and preferences you create are stored only on your own device (in the app’s local storage). They are never transmitted to us or to anyone else, and we cannot see them. Deleting the app removes this data.

Network use

  • Mobile and watch apps: designed to work fully offline. They make no network calls to us and send no data off your device.
  • Web app: when opened in a browser, the page may request the “Outfit” web font from Google Fonts. That request is handled by Google and is subject to Google’s privacy policy. The mobile apps bundle the font locally and make no such request.

Purchases

TapScore Pro is an optional one-time in-app purchase. Payments are processed entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play — we never see or receive your payment details. Whether you have purchased Pro is recorded on your device and, where the store supports it, tied to your store account so you can restore it. These transactions are governed by Apple’s and Google’s privacy policies.

Device features we use

  • Bluetooth clicker (optional): if you pair a Bluetooth remote/clicker, it is paired at the operating-system level. TapScore only receives the button presses as input — it does not access your Bluetooth settings, device list, or any other data.
  • Voice announcements (optional): spoken scores use your device’s built-in text-to-speech. No audio is recorded and the microphone is never used.
  • Keep-awake: the app may keep the screen on during a match. No data is involved.

TapScore does not request access to your location, camera, microphone, contacts, or files.

Children’s privacy

TapScore is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal data from anyone, we collect no data from children.

Third parties

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only third parties involved are the app stores that distribute TapScore and process purchases (Apple, Google) and, for the web app only, Google Fonts. We do not sell or share any data — because we do not have any to sell or share.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
